Text-base Product Card

Products are essentially lists

"checkout" is the action

{{#storefront-products 
  collection=model 
  banner="Don't miss this chance" 
  fineprint="All prices are per person except as noted above."
  as |product|}}
  {{#storefront-textbase-product checkout="endOfPreview" model=product as |yields|}}
    {{#yields.title}}{{product.name}} - {{product.displayPrice}}{{/yields.title}}
    {{#yields.subtitle}}{{product.description}}{{/yields.subtitle}}
    {{#yields.cta}}<i class="material-icons">add_shopping_cart</i>{{/yields.cta}}
  {{/storefront-textbase-product}}
{{/storefront-products}}
